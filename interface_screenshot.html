How to Use:

  • Tool Selection: Click on rectangular buttons on the left side
  • Color Selection: Click on color rectangles in the 4x4 grid on the right
  • Drawing: Click and drag in the large drawing area in the center
  • Visual Feedback: Active tools are highlighted, current color has white border
